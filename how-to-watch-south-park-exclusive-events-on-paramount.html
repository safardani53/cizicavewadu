    What is a South Park exclusive event?
    A South Park exclusive event is content that is available to stream only on Paramount+. South Park co-creators Trey Parker and Matt Stone are executive producers. South Park characters you know, love and quote extensively are on hand to entertain in all their uncensored glory.

    How many South Park exclusive events are there?
    As of publication, there are five South Park exclusive events available to stream now with Paramount. They are: SOUTH PARK: POST COVID; SOUTH PARK: POST COVID: THE RETURN OF COVID; ​​SOUTH PARK THE STREAMING WARS PART 1; SOUTH PARK THE STREAMING WARS PART 2; and, SOUTH PARK: JOINING THE PANDEVERSE, which premiered on the service on Friday, Oct. 27.

    What is SOUTH PARK: JOINING THE PANDEVERSE about?
    In SOUTH PARK: JOINING THE PANDEVERSE, now streaming exclusively on Paramount+, Cartman’s deeply disturbing dreams portend the end of the life he knows and loves. The adults in South Park are also wrestling with their own life decisions as the advent of AI is turning their world upside down.

    What is SOUTH PARK: POST COVID about?
    In SOUTH PARK: POST COVID, now streaming exclusively on Paramount+, we’ll find out the answer to the question: what happened to the children who lived through the pandemic? Stan, Kyle, Cartman and Kenny survived, but will never be the same post COVID.

    What is SOUTH PARK: POST COVID: THE RETURN OF COVID about?
    If Stan, Kyle and Cartman could just work together, they could go back in time to make sure Covid never happened and save Kenny’s life. In SOUTH PARK: POST COVID: THE RETURN OF COVID, now streaming exclusively on Paramount+, traveling back to the past seems to be the easy answer until they meet Victor Chaos.

    In SOUTH PARK THE STREAMING WARS PART 1, now streaming exclusively on Paramount+, Cartman locks horns with his mom in a battle of wills, while an epic conflict unfolds and threatens South Park’s very existence.

    What is SOUTH PARK THE STREAMING WARS PART 2 about?
    In SOUTH PARK THE STREAMING WARS PART 2, now streaming exclusively on Paramount+, a drought has brought the town of South Park to the brink of disaster.

    Can I only watch South Park exclusive events on Paramount+?
    Yes, South Park exclusive events, including the just-released SOUTH PARK: JOINING THE PANDEVERSE, stream exclusively on Paramount+.
