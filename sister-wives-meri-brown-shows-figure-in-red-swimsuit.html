    She reveals that it's her "last day" on vacation, as she turns her camera to show off her view and give fans a glimpse of the luxurious resort's exterior.

    Meri then turns the camera back on herself, the 51-year-old wears a cherry red swimsuit that features a see-through mesh upper around her neck.
