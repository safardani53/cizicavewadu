    Derek Hough Bio: Age, Parents, Early Life

    Derek Hough was born on 17 May 1985 in Salt Lake City, Utah in the United States of America. He was the fourth of five children in his family, with father Bruce Hough and mother Marianne Hough.

    His father became the chairman of Utah Republican Party twice. His parents divorced when he was just 12 years old.

    Derek's Career; Dancing & Acting; Net Worth

    33-year-old Derek pursued a dancing career from a young age. He was sent to London at the age of 12 to study dance under coaches. He stayed at the city for ten years and attended Italia Conti Academy of Theatre Arts.

    Derek appeared in the fourth season of Dancing With The Stars and onwards became a professional instructor. He has till date appeared as a pro dancer in the show for 13 seasons, and won five of the season with his respective partner. 

    He also won an Emmy Award for Outstanding Choreography for his dedication in the show. Aside, from being a regular choreographer in the show, Ferek also had his hands in the acting realm. 

    Back in 2006, he appeared in the Footloose: The Musical in London. He was also going to star in ABC's series Cougar Town, but his hectic schedule in the DWTS made him step away from the project. 

    With decades-long work in the dance world, Derek, who was born on 17 May 1985, has amassed a net worth of $4 million. Moreover, he has also earned fame and been high to stardom with his success.
