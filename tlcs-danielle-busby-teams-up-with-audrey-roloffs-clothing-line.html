    Teaming Up With Audrey Roloff Clothing Line!

    TLC favorites are crossing over!

    On March 7, the OutDaughtered star Danielle Busby took to Instagram to announce to her fans that she is partnering with Always More, a clothing company started by former Little People, Big World star Audrey Roloff.

    And we’re cheering hard. 

    "One thing is for sure, this @shopalwaysmore NEW ROSE SHERPA is a.m.a.z.i.n.g!" Danielle wrote while modeling the fluffy sweatshirt. 

    "It literally is the softest out of all my sherpas I own."

    She concluded her post by sharing her personal code: danielle15 for her fans to receive a discount. 

    And she also, of course, tagged Audrey and her husband Jeremy Roloff in the pic. 

    It took the new mom approximately three seconds to chime in, writing "LOVEEEEEEE" with three heart emojis in the comment section. 

    Her sister-in-law even shared her support, writing "get it girl!" to the mother of 6.

    But the fans brought the most excitement, writing, "My two favorite worlds colliding!!!!"

    Another agreed, saying, "Love @shopalwaysmore!! And I love watching two amazingly strong women like you @dbusby and @audreyroloff!"

    Audrey introduced her Always More brand via her blog where she shared the meaning behind the words.

    "Over the years I have kept every pair of running shoes that I have ever had. I spray paint them red, write notes on/in them, say a prayer, and throw them on telephone wires near places significant to my life," she wrote.

    "It has become my way of letting go of a certain season in my life, and embracing a new one."

    "On the bottom of every pair I write "ALWAYS MORE," she continued. "These words represent the way I strive to live, based on my motives for living that way."

    This isn’t the first member of the TLC fam to show their love towards Audrey’s multiple ventures. 

    Just three weeks earlier, Jinger Duggar from Counting On posted a sweet photo with her hubby Jeremy Vuolo with the message, "He is everything I asked God for in a husband – he loves Christ supremely, is loving, compassionate, caring, humble, and sincere."

    "Every day with you is sweeter than the day before – I love you, baby! #3sentencelovestory @beating50percent."

    Beating 50 Percent is another one of Audrey’s passion projects which is all about beating the divorce rate statistics and staying married no matter what, so totally appropriate for the devoted Duggar fam. 

    And while Danielle is most likely receiving a commission for every item she sells using her code, Jinger seems to be simply a fan of the concept of Beating 50 Percent. 

    But there’s nothing at all wrong with Danielle’s position, I mean, she does have six children at home to feed and we totally back the duo’s partnership.

    Fans were shocked to learn of the somewhat recent news that Audrey and Jeremy Roloff were leaving the show Little People, Big World.

    However, I’m sure they can rest easy knowing that the two aren’t completely shutting TLC out of their life, considering the remaining friendly support.

    We think it’s great the moms are teaming up and we hope to see even more from them in the future!
