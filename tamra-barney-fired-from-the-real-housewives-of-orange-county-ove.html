    FIRED From The Real Housewives of Orange County Over Facebook Post?!

    Tamra Barney sealed her fate on The Real Housewives of Orange County after going on a epic rant against Bravo this week, according to a new report. As a result, insiders say she will not be back for the shows 10th season.

    01 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Tamra Barney sealed her fate on The Real Housewives of Orange County after going on a epic rant against Bravo this week, according to a new report.

    As a result, insiders say she will not be back for the show’s 10th season.

    “The network has had it with Tamra’s ego and diva behavior. She sealed her fate by publicly stating that Bravo edited scenes to make her look bad,” a source said.

    “This is a very big no-no, and Tamra’s contract won’t be picked up.”

    “The fact that she has ongoing legal issues with ex-husband and really doesn’t have anything that interesting going on in her life made it a very easy decision.”

    Ouch. Earlier this week, Tamra Barney slammed Bravo for editing scenes to create fake drama, specifically making it look like she flaked on Lizzie Rovsek’s party.

    For her part, Lizzie Rovsek says that Tamra is the real liar here.

    Barney also has appeared desperate for a child with new husband Eddie Judge this season, but in reality doesn’t want more kids, and has had her tubes tied.

    How did that fact mysteriously leak? You be the Judge.

    While reports of Bravo axing Tamra have made the media rounds before, this time it looks like it could be legit, and that the decision is mutual.

    The 46-year-old star has already deleted her suddenly infamous Facebook page and left the U.S. for a Mexican vacation of undetermined length.

    “One more day till paradise,” Barney tweeted on July 29 (her Twitter is still active). “I’m so over the stress of the show and what it’s doing to my spirit.”

    At least she still has her looks after all these years …
