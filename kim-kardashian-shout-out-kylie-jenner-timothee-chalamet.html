    KIM Kardashian has given a shout-out to sister Kylie Jenner's boyfriend, Timothee Chalamet, in a new video amid breakup rumors.

    In an Instagram Story, Kim, 43, gave followers a glimpse at her unclean bathroom.

    She teased her four young children - North, 10; Saint, eight; Chicago, five; and Psalm, four - played a prank on their mother.

    In the brief clip, The Kardashians star pointed the camera to the lit Christmas trees in the background to the low table connected to a square bathtub.

    On the surface of the table, a bunch of crushed-up candy spelled the word: "Elf."

    The Hulu star mockingly said: "The elves left a mess."

    Kim concluded: "This is crazy! This is Willy Wonka!"

    Throughout the short video, Kim made references to Timothee's latest film, Wonka.

    The new musical is out in theaters now.

    She shares her four kids with her ex-husband, Kanye West, 46.

    BREAKING UP?

    Kim's post came after fans speculated the Little Women actor split with her sibling, Kylie, 26, 

    In mid-December, breakup rumors sparked when Kylie left a fancy restaurant in Los Angeles, California, with her sister, Khloe Kardashian, 38, and mother, Kris Jenner, 68. 

    The Kardashian clan hosted a festive party at Casa Vega.

    The mom of two was spotted outside the event while modeling a shimmery red two-piece ensemble.

    Kylie's short skirt flaunted her curvy and toned legs as she wore a long-sleeved jacket.

    The matriarch dressed in an all-black look, which included her gloves and a small handbag.

    After getting a good look at Kylie's phone, eagle-eyed fans theorized the relationship between Kylie and Timothee hit a rocky road.

    In the past, Kylie had a snapshot of her boyfriend as her phone's background.

    Now, Kylie's lock screen is a snap of her children: Stormi, five, and Aire Webster, one - she shares both with ex-baby daddy Travis Scott, 32.
