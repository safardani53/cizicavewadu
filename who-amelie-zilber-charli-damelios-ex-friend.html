    MODEL, Amelie Zilber, is more than just a pretty face.

    The 20-year-old activist is a Young Ambassador for UNICEF.

    Who is Amelie Zilber?

    Amelie Zilber is a Los Angeles native born on March 27, 2002.

    Being that she is part French and part Lebanese, Zilber speaks fluent French and is in the process of learning Arabic.

    She started modeling at the early age of 15 and is currently signed by the LA Models agency.

    As per IMDb, she has modeled for Dior and Ralph Lauren and has appeared in publications including Vogue, TeenVogue, Harpers Bazaar, and more.

    Read More on TikTok

    Zilber, however, is also known as an activist to her over 2.5million followers on Instagram.

    Her interest in politics - particularly Middle Eastern politics - lead her to create a newsletter called Two Minute Times that breaks down news stories and makes them easy to understand.

    "I created the 2MT when I realized most of my friends knew very little about the news, and have little patience to sift through pages of often long and unclear news articles," Zilber explains on her website.

    "I write weekly newsletters so you can understand today’s global issues without being overwhelmed. In my spare time, I can be found on face time marathons with friends, researching away, and, oh yah, modeling with LA Models too."

    Most read in Celebrity

    Zilber is currently attending Georgetown University and is a Young Ambassador for UNICEF.

    In an interview with Seventeen, she explained the importance of demolishing the stereotype that pretty girls cannot be smart girls.

    "I do think it's important that girls who are beautiful and confident and love themselves can also be smart and intelligent and empowering," she told the outlet.

    "I think that's something, a stereotype that we need to break and we need to keep working on. Can we just freaking normalize that beautiful girls can be really freaking smart and empowering? Let's normalize that because a lot of us are."

    What show is Amelie Zilber featured in?

    In addition to her long list of talents, Zilber is also an actress.

    The fashionista landed her first acting job in 2022 on Freeform’s Grown-ish.

    Prior to the show, Zilber had no acting experience and had to enroll in acting classes five to six days a week for 12 hours a week, according to ABC News.

    Zilber - who plays Lauryn Daniels in Grown-ish - is described as a "natural talent" and a "delightful new comic energy" by the show's executive producer and co-showrunner, Zakiyyah Alexander.

    Who is Amelie Zilber dating?

    The model-turned-actress has been dating TikTok star, Blake Grey for two years.

    The pair celebrated their relationship milestone on September 10, 2022, with Zilber posting a slew of pictures with her boo on Instagram to commemorate the occasion.

    She captioned the post: "Thank you for always letting me choose where to eat and what to watch. 2 years is a big deal !!! I love you."
