    Teen Mom star Cory Wharton announces girlfriend Taylor Selfridge is PREGNANT in emotional post

    TEEN Mom star Cory Wharton announced that his girlfriend Taylor Selfridge is pregnant with his third child in an emotional post while celebrating his birthday. Cory, 31, made the sweet announcement in an Instagram post.

    02 Sep 2024, 00:00

    TEEN Mom star Cory Wharton announced that his girlfriend Taylor Selfridge is pregnant with his third child in an emotional post while celebrating his birthday.

    Cory, 31, made the sweet announcement in an Instagram post.

    The Teen Mom OG star wrote: "We will welcome a new member to the family! I couldn’t think of a better way to spend my 31st birthday.

    "Each one of my kids has continued to push me into making me a better man, so I know you’re gonna do the same."

    The MTV dad continued: "Throughout the years the satisfaction I get from raising my two little girls is unmatched. The joy that they bring me, the excitement is unmatched.

    "I truthfully feel like God has put me in a position that I’m so lucky & blessed to be in. As a kid, my dad wasn’t able to be around, and I feel like that’s why I try and give you girls EVERYTHING that I have."

    The reality star then spoke about his daughters: "I can’t wait to watch you grow with your sisters Ryder and Mila. I’m telling you right now both those girls love you so much."

    He mentioned his baby mama next: "I don’t know too many people to put up with me for as long as you have.

    "But no seriously, I love you baby & I can’t wait to start this adventure with you and continue to build this family up."

    Cory concluded: "I also wanna say thank you to my support system those people know who you are just know I love you all and I appreciate it everything you do for me."

    A BIGGER FAMILY

    The Teen Mom star and his girlfriend are expecting their child to be born on June 8th.

    In the lovely group photo, Taylor, 27, was holding onto the black-and-white image of the ultrasound.

    He shares his four-year-old daughter with his ex Cheyenne Floyd, 28.

    Cory and Taylor - who he met on Ex On The Beach - are the parents of their one-year-old daughter Mila.

    Cory captioned the post: "It started with us."

    The snap resurfaced on Reddit, where some fans theorized that Cory's caption was hinting that he is about to become a father of three.
