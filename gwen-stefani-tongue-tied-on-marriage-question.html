    03 Mar 2024, 00:00

    Gwen Stefani’s 2016 has been sprinkled with glitter and rainbows.

    "I look at this year and I think, I just feel so awake," she told Ryan Seacrest during an On Air interview.

    Not for nothing, but the No Doubt frontwoman deserves it. 

    After enduring the most craptastic year of her life, Stefani filed for divorce from Gavin Rossdale in August 2015.  A few months later, she joined The Voice as a judge and started dating her co-star, Blake Shelton.

    Since then, Stefani’s life has been nothing short of magical, what with her romance and This Is What The Truth Feels Like tour.

    Without referring directly to her relationship, Stefani told Seacrest that she’s a little bummed out that the year is winding down.

    "I feel so alive and so grateful and I promise you, the last couple of days of the tour when I knew it was coming to an end…

    "I was so depressed because this year has been so full of so much joy, so much gratitude, and all the creativity that came out of it," Stefani said.

    Yes, yes, Seacrest thought, switching topics to a little rumor that Stefani and Shelton had hired party planner Jerri Woolworth to organize a special event.

    Will she wed again?

    "I don’t know! God, I don’t know. Everything is going so quickly. I’m in the moment and um –," Stefani paused, unable to give Seacrest a straight annswer.

    "Look at me! You got me tongue-tied." 

    The wedding planner rumor started in early August, according to People Magazine.

    The bit of news started "circulating in their circle that they’ve begun preparations for a wedding," a source told the publication.

    "There’s no date set, but they’re planning ahead and having fun talking about it."

    To be clear, Shelton had not (at that point) proposed to Stefani, nor has he done so…yet.

    "Everyone is expecting it to happen at some point, but Gwen is touring and busy," the source explained.

    "The time just hasn’t been right."

    There are some rumors that build up over time to the point where they may just be true.  

    Shelton and Stefani’s relationship has been drama-free since they started dating, so it would surprise no one to find out that they want to make it official.
