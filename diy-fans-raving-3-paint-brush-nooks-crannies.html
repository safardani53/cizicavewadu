    GETTING into to all the small nooks and crannies when you're painting your own home can be a bit of a challenge.

    But DIY fans have the perfect solution, and it will only set you back £3.

    The appropriately named Nook and Cranny Brush from Wilko has a long handle and an angled brush so you can get to those hard to reach places.

    DIY expert Claire Douglas raved about the bargain brush on Instagram, and she's not the only one obsessed with it.

    She found the brush to have multiple uses, including precision and painting places you'd usually need a ladder to reach.

    "I was surprised how handy this £3 brush turned out to be for painting hard to reach places," she wrote in the caption of the clip.
