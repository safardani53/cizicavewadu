    For Kanye West, there is no greater inspiration than the famous booty of his fiancée, Kim Kardashian. 

    West got lyrical about the reality star’s backside in his verse on singer Future‘s new song, “I Won.” Out Tuesday, April 8, the track has Yeezus and Future celebrating their lady companions, Kardashian, 33, and R&B singer Ciara, 28, respectively, comparing the women to trophies. 

    “You the number one trophy wife / so it’s only right to live the trophy life,” West raps about his betrothed. “You grew up on J. Lo, Timberlands by Manolo now / Till one day I put an angel in your ultra sound.”

    West gets romantic by referencing Kardashian’s derriere and her very public dating history, continuing, “I want to dip that ass in gold, I want to dip that ass in gold / I made it over NBA, NFL players / So every time I score it’s like the Super Bowl. Baby, we should hit the south of France / So you could run around without them pants.”
