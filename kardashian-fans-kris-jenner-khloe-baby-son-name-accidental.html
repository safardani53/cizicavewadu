    KARDASHIAN fans have predicted that Khloé will finally reveal the name of her baby boy in the season finale next week.

    At the end of episode nine of The Kardashians, viewers got a glimpse at the tenth and final instalment and it seems to focus on Khloé's newborn son.

    The Good American founder welcomed her baby boy with Tristan Thompson via surrogate in July 2022, but has kept his name a secret.

    In a teaser clip for the season finale of the popular Hulu series, Khloé is seen fussing over her son before handing him over to her mom Kris Jenner.

    The youngster's face is hidden in the scene but fans get to see how tiny he is dressed in a gray onesie and a matching hat.

    In her confessional, Kris is heard saying to the camera: "Life is good. A new baby in the family is magic, he's such a blessing."

    Khloé's older sister Kim Kardashian is also present and asks: "What are we gonna name him? That's what I need to know."

    The credits then rolled before fans got the chance to hear Khloé's reply.

    Viewers immediately began to speculate that they will find out the name in the season finale episode next week.

    Posting the clip on a Kardashians Instagram fan page, one person said: "I think we are getting a name reveal on next week’s season finale of The Kardashians!"

    Referring to Kris' comment in particular, one fan suggested: "Was that a hint? 'Blessing'," while another said: "A new baby in the family is magic.. maybe his name is magic ! lol."

    A third added: "Magic is the name. I bettcha ???" while another simply asked: "Magic?"

    KHLOE'S HEARTACHE

    The arrival of the little boy has been somewhat of an emotional rollercoaster for Khloe amid her ex Tristan’s infidelity scandal.

    The 31-year-old NBA star welcomed a child with his mistress Maralee Nichols in December 2021 after he cheated on Khloé with the fitness model earlier that year.

    But Khloe had already decided to have another baby via a surrogate with Tristan before she found out about the paternity scandal via leaked out documents.

    'HEALING PROCESS'

    This is the closest look fans have had at her son since Khloe finally revealed his face on the season two premiere.

    Viewers saw the first time that Khloe got to hold her son in a tender moment at the hospital.

    Despite her baby daddy drama, she seemed relieved to finally have her son in her ams.

    The new mom-of-two gushed in a confessional: "I am so grateful. It's such a beautiful gift that we're able to have.
