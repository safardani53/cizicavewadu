    Netflix’s new drama Partner Track will premiere on the platform on August 26, 2022, at 12 a.m. PT/3 a.m. ET. The story follows a young lawyer as she works her way to the top of the business world, facing numerous hurdles. The sitcom, created by Georgia Lee, stars Arden Cho, Dominic Sherwood, and Alexandra Turshen, among many more. Continue reading to learn more about the Netflix drama.

    Partner Track is based on a well-known book.

    The first season will have 10 episodes, all of which will air on the same day. The series is based on the renowned book The Partner Track by recognized novelist Helen Wan. The first two episodes of the program will allegedly be directed by Julie Anne Robinson. On July 29, 2022, Netflix released the series’ official trailer, which sets the basic narrative by showing a confident Arden Cho with a voice-over that states,

    “Being on the relationship track teaches you who you truly are. It’s competitive, and I’m so close.”

    The trailer follows the main heroine, Ingrid Yun, as she faces different personal and professional problems. The teaser has a lively tone that lovers of fast-paced, character-driven dramas will definitely like. Viewers should anticipate a truly enjoyable series full of intriguing and engaging characters. According to the official Netflix synopsis:

    “At a top New York legal firm, Ingrid Yun works to become a partner — and to keep her ideals — while juggling romance, friends, and family expectations.”

    More information about the Partner Track cast

    Arden Cho plays the primary character of Ingrid Yun in the program. She is a young, ambitious lawyer attempting to climb the corporate ladder while battling to sacrifice her ethics and moral values. Cho appears extremely amazing in the clip, as she depicts her character’s myriad subtle hues with surprising ease. This really gifted actress will provide a great performance. Cho has featured in a number of films and television series throughout the years, including Teen Wolf, Tween Fest, Chicago Med, and Stuck.

    Jeff Murphy, another charming and skilled lawyer at the company, is played by Dominic Sherwood. Rachel Friedman is played by Alexandra Turshen. Aside from the performers named above, the series also stars a number of others in key roles, including:

    • Tyler Robinson is played by Bradley Gibson.
    • Nick Laren is played by Rob Heaps.
    • Marty Adler is played by Matthew Rauch.
    • Nolan Dan Fallon is played by Gerard Funk.

    Georgie Lee conceived the program, and she also acts as an executive producer with co-showrunner Sarah Goldfinger. Lee is most recognized for his work on The Expanse, Red Doors, and The 100, while Goldfinger is best known for her work on Netflix’s adolescent drama Trinkets. Partner Track will be available on Netflix on Friday, August 26, 2022.
