    THE newest thriller, Last Seen Alive, will be packed with a star-studded cast.

    The film is set to premiere in the Summer of 2022.

    Last Seen Alive: Who is in the Cast?

    On Wednesday, April 20, 2022, the official trailer for the film, Last Seen Alive, dropped.

    Here is what we know about the cast:

    Gerard Butler 

    Gerard Butler, 52, will play the main character of Will Spann in Last Seen Alive.

    Butler is a Scottish actor and producer who has starred in over 50 films and numerous television shows.

    Read More on The US Sun

    Jaimie Alexander

    Jaimie Alexander, 38, will play the main character, Lisa Spann, Will's wife in the new thriller.

    Alexander is a native of South Carolina, but grew up in Texas.

    She moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career in acting and is known for her roles in Kyle XY and Marvel's Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.

    Most read in Entertainment

    Dani Deetté 

    Dani Deetté will star as Sgt Andersen.

    She is most known for her recurring roles as Gigi on Marvel's The Falcon and The Winter Soldier.

    Robert Walker Branchaud 

    Robert Walker Branchaud will star as Jo Paul.

    He is an actor and producer, known for his roles in Hawkeye (2021), The Walking Dead (2010) and Stranger Things (2016), according to IMDb.

    David Kallaway 

    David Kallaway will star as Frank in the film.

    He is known for his role as, Ray, in the Academy Award-winning film Green Book.

    Russell Hornsby 

    Russell Hornsby, 47, will also star in Last Seen Alive.

    He is known for playing one of the leading roles, Maverick Carter, in The Hate U Give.

    Other cast members include Jordan Salloum, Alphonso A'Qen-Aten Jackson and Aleks Alifrenko Jr.

    When is the release date?

    Last Seen Alive is set to premiere on June 3, 2022.

    It will be Brian Goodman's third film as a director.

    Goodman previously directed, What Doesn’t Kill You, in 2008 and, Black Butterfly, in 2017.
