    BREAKFAST lovers have begged Kellogg's to bring back an old favorite that is full of noughties nostalgia.

    The cereal company discontinued its Eggo Waf-FULLS years ago but this has not stopped loyal fans from begging for their return.

    The toaster waffles contained a jelly that was available in a number of flavors including strawberry, syrup, and chocolate.

    One fan tweeted the company in April: "@KelloggsUS Could you bring back Waf-FULLS?"

    Kellogg's replied: "Unfortunately, those were discontinued because they did not have enough fans.

    "We're sorry to disappoint but appreciate your interest. Thanks for being a loyal fan."

    read more on discontinued items

    Another Waf-FULLS fan replied to the company saying: "That's the beauty of social media Kellogg's.

    "Fans like me can help bring those discontinued items back."

    While the breakfast food company believes that there were not enough fans to keep the product, social media tells a different story.

    "Waf-FULLS were THE best invention I JUST WANT HER BACK," one Twitter user wrote.

    Most read in Money

    Another said: "I can’t be the only person who still thinks about the Waf-fulls…"

    "They were very good, and they should probably exist again," a third wrote.

    Meanwhile, others took a trip down memory lane, saying that the breakfast product is still close to their hearts.

    One said: "They are one of my fondest memories of my childhood. A food item that I still hold dear to my heart many, many, many years later.

    "I have not, and will not, Leggo my Eggo Strawberry Waf-Fulls."

    Another fan even said that they try to convince Kellogg's to bring them back every single year.

    "Every year I write Kellogg's, asking for the return of my favorite frozen food product," they said.

    "As I approach my thirties, nostalgia is flooding through my veins, and the memory of Waf-Fulls is at the forefront of my mind."

    As a child, one Twitter user said that they would eat an entire box before storming the basketball court.

    "I haven’t been truly happy since @eggo discontinued the Waf-Fulls.

    "Young Trill would go through a box of them on a Saturday morning without batting an eye, and then drop 14 & 8 for my Upwards basketball team," he said.

    There is even a Change.org petition called "Bring back Eggo Waf-Fulls!!" in a bid to prove that social media has the power to bring them back.

    The petition has almost reached its goal of 100 signatures, with 93 currently having signed the petition.

    Petition launcher, Bobby McLaughlin started the page in August 2019, saying the was "saddened when they got discontinued."

    Meanwhile, Kellogg's has also revealed that its Fruit Harvest cereal has also been axed due to low sales, which further disappointed customers.
