    What happened at the Verzuz rap battle with Bone Thugs-N-Harmony?

    27 Jun 2024, 00:00

    THREE 6 Mafia and Bone Thugs-N-Harmony hit the stage in Los Angeles for the newest Verzuz rap battle.

    However, during their battle, a fight allegedly broke out between Three 6 Mafia and Bone Thugs-N-Harmony, nearly derailing the entire event.

    What happened at the Verzuz rap battle with Bone Thugs-N-Harmony?

    On Thursday, December 2, 2021, hip-hop groups Three 6 Mafia and Bone Thugz-N-Harmony competed live from the Hollywood Palladium in California.

    However, things allegedly heated up in the fourth round, ending in a massive brawl, according to video footage shared on YouTube. 

    After Bone Thugs completed playing the rap song Buddah Lovaz, an altercation ensued on stage.

    Bizzy Bone allegedly seemed enraged by DJ Paul and Gangsta Boo mockingly slow dancing during his group’s performance.

    He addressed Three 6 after the song before allowing them to continue, saying: “Hey yo, hey. Before we even get started, you ugly mother******* ain’t fit to be mocking me while I’m on the mother******* stage. Like straight the f*** up.”

    According to Rolling Stone, the groups and their respective security came together and allegedly began throwing punches, temporarily turning the event into a full-fledged riot.

    Was the brawl resolved?

    The groups reunited a few minutes later, absent Bizzy Bone, with Three 6 Mafia preserving the peace with Azz N' Titties and Bone Thugs countering with Days Of Our Lives.

    Bizzy, on the other hand, reportedly ended up embracing Juicy J later and called for peace: “I want to apologize to everybody the f*** out there, on both sides.

    "I’m not trying to f*** this s*** up. “Pardon me. Let’s keep the party mother******* going.” 

    Guests Lil Wayne and Lil Jon made cameos in a brighter section of the evening as the performers cooled down.

    Who are Bone Thugs-N-Harmony and Three 6 Mafia?

    Bone Thugs-N-Harmony is a hip-hop group from the US, whose members are Bizzy Bone, Wish Bone, Layzie Bone, Krayzie Bone, and Flesh-n-Bone.

    When Bone Thugs-N-Harmony emerged with their EP Creepin on ah Come Up in late 1993, American West Coast rapper Eazy-E signed them to Ruthless Records. Thuggish Ruggish Bone, their first smash track, was included on the EP.

    Similarly, Three 6 Mafia is a Memphis, Tennessee-based hip-hop trio.

    Gangsta Blac, Gangsta Boo, La' Chat, Koopsta Knicca, Lord Infamous, Playa Fly, and Crunchy Black were among the original members of the group.

    Juicy J and DJ Paul, who also act as in-house producers, are the only members of the group left.

    Mystic Stylez, the first official album by then-named Triple 6 Mafia, was released in 1995.

    It had recorded this album with the proceeds from their popular underground album Smoked Out, Loced Out.
