    Tom Brady Takes His Parents Out for a Las Vegas Date Night

    12 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Tom Brady hit the Las Vegas strip with his parents on Thursday, taking them out for a date night at the opening of the long-awaited Fontainebleau hotel.

    The invite-only grand opening event was headlined with a special performance by Justin Timberlake and attended by A-list stars like Brady, Cher, Kim Kardashian, Lenny Kravitz, and more. The luxury hotel officially opens its doors to the public on Thursday — nearly two decades after the sister property to the iconic Fontainebleau Miami was first announced.

    Brady, 46, appeared to have a great time at the event, sharing more than a dozen photos of himself posing with family and friends throughout the night, including back-to-back photos of his parents Tom Brady Sr. and Galynn Patricia smiling and posing for the camera.

    “The Brady Bunch minus 1!” the seven-time Super Bowl champion wrote in one of his Instagram stories, sharing a photo of him sitting around a table with most of his family, minus Brady’s sister Nancy. “We miss you Nancy!” he added.

    Brady also shared a photo with longtime friend and billionaire businessman Jeffrey Soffer, writing “The BOSS” over the photo. In the next slide, Brady then shared another image of his parents and captioned it, “The other bosses!” with a heart emoji.

    Brady largely keeps his family life underwraps, but in 2016, he shared that Galynn was diagnosed with cancer and underwent five months of chemotherapy, according to CBS Sports.

    In 2017, Brady's father, Tom Brady Sr., said that his wife was a "cancer survivor," and in 2018, the Tampa Bay Buccaneers superstar told PEOPLE that his mother was, "doing really well." But he added, "I think any family that has dealt with cancer realizes the challenge it is not only at that moment, but as it continues to be."

    The retired NFL legend has kept his social life busy since stepping away from football for good in February.

    The three-time NFL MVP was linked earlier this year to model Irina Shayk after the pair were spotted at billionaire art heir Joe Nahmad's wedding to model Madison Headrick in Sardinia, Italy in July. Later that month, paparazzi caught Brady and Shayk in the car together after she spent the night at a Los Angeles home where he was also staying.

    A source told PEOPLE at the time there was a “spark” between the two, but by October PEOPLE had learned the pair’s relationship had fizzled.

    Last week, however, Brady and Shayk were spotted together again in Miami, raising questions about whether the pair were dating again. Shayk was not spotted at the Vegas event Thursday night and instead shared a number of photos and videos of herself on Instagram at a carnival.
