    Lidl is selling a perfume dupe of Dior's famous J'adore scent and it's 115 cheaper

    LOOKING for a new perfume that makes a statement but strapped for cash?

    Need a last minute gift for a friend or relative but don't have the funds to spare?

    Shoppers are racing to Lidl grab their latest round of perfume dupes, including a scent similar to Dior J'adore for £115 cheaper.

    The must-have dupes were highlighted by a video posted to the @dontmesswiththedupes TikTok channel, which often showcases the best high street dupes of designer brands.

    The channel flagged that budget supermarket Lidl is currently selling a copy of Dior J'adore, which currently retails at £122.

    The Lidl version of the perfume, Lovely, is just £5.25 for a 75ml bottle.

    Both products have the same floral scent with notes of orange and jasmine, so you can smell great without breaking the bank.

    The TikTok also highlighted that Lidl is currently selling a dupe of Lancome's £96 perfume, La Vie Est Belle.

    Lidl's Femelle perfume is only £5.25 for a 75ml bottle and has a similar floral and fruity scent, so you're friends will never know the difference.

    The bargain supermarket is also selling a dupe of Chloé, which currently retails at £90 for just £5.25.

    Chatou has a similar floral scent to its designer counterpart, so you can treat yourself without having to splash out.

    Lidl is also currently selling dupes on men's aftershave, including a copy of Paco Rabanne's £72 fragrance, 1 Million.

    One Fragrance which is also just £5.25, has a similar cinnamony scent, so is a great budget gift for your partner or brother.

    Lidl shoppers can also get their hands on a dupe of an £81 bottle of Bleu De Chanel, again for just £5.25.

    Deep has a similar woody scent to its designer counterpart so would again be a great gift for the man in your life.
