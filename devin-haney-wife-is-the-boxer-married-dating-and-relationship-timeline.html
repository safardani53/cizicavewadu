    Is Devin Haney’s wife Leena Sayed? Is Devin Haney married? These are the questions that have gripped the fans of the American boxer.

    These past few months have been a whirlwind for the boxer, especially in his love life. From breaking up with his girlfriend of two years, India Love, to getting abruptly married in the mecca, Haney isn’t playing around.

    The lightweight champion, Haney, is the undisputed champion, having never been defeated in his entire career.

    Since making his debut in 2017 at age 15, Haney hasn’t looked back.

    In his sensational 29-run winning streak, he has defeated boxers, including Joseph Diaz and Jorge Linares.

    The California-raised fighter is looking to maintain his winning streak against Vasiliy Lomachenko.

    On May 20, 2023, the two will square up against each other for the first time at MGM Grand in Las Vegas, Nevada.

    Haney has already taken shots against the Ukrainian boxer, saying he is better than the Ukrainian boxer in every aspect.

    Is Devin Haney Married? Is Leena Sayed His Wife?

    Devin Haney might have made Leena Sayed his wife during his latest trip to Saudi Arabia. 

    At least, that is what we can say from the video circulating on the internet. It hadn’t been long since Leena and Devin got to know each other, but the boxer might have felt he had found the right person. 

    A TikTok video shows Devin and Leena inside Mecca, exchanging their vows. In the video, a man makes the couple read their vows, starting with Leena, who repeats the vows, “I, Leena, have offered myself in nikah to Devin.”

    The boxer says the same to Leena. But many fans aren’t sure whether this marriage between Leena and Devin can be considered legal.

    Many fans have pointed out that an unmarried couple cannot make a spiritual journey to Mecca before marriage.

    So, many fans believe this is all for Instagram and Devin, using it to make his former girlfriend, India, jealous.

    Who Is Devin Haney, Wife Leena Sayed?

    Leena Sayed, the rumored wife of Devin Haney, is an entrepreneur, and a model, with a sizeable following on social media handles.

    The gorgeous brunette never fails to set the pulses racing with her stunning pictures. She is available on Instagram with the username @leenasayed and also has a personal website. 

    The rumored wife-to-be of Devin Haney also has an Onlyfans account and is now looking forward to opening an art collection. 

    The better half of Devin Haney was one of the attendees of the fight between Israel Adesanya and Alex Pereira, having shared a photo of her during the game on her handle. 

    Devin Haney Former Relationship With India Love

    The love story of Devin Haney and India Love was one for the ages. 

    India Love, age 27, is an American rapper, social media influencer, and model. The multi-faceted came to media attention in 2015 from the show “The Westbrooks” which followed the life of five sisters.

    After stepping into the entertainment industry at a young age, Love also faced several online abuses and harassment, which she has talked about in the past. 

    Devin and India went public with their romance in 2021 but had known each other way before. The boxer, from his Instagram story, confirmed the fact that he had known India since he was 16/17. 

    After going public with their romance, India started to be seen with Devin more frequently than ever. On one occasion, India was in the boxing ring to celebrate Devin’s victory with him. 

    In late 2022, the rapper even got the name Devin tatted on her right arm. A move that left most of her fans questioning why she did it. 

    What seemed to be turning into a serious relationship fizzled out right as 2023 began. In March 2023, The Shade Room reached out to India to confirm whether she had broken up with Devin.

    The social media influencer said the rumors were true and that she is no longer in a relationship with the boxer. At the end of the conversation, India wished well for Devin and his future.
