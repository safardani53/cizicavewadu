    A SPONGE designed to "clean up" bodily fluids after sex now exists.

    It's certainly not sexy, but most women will be familiar with the messy and somewhat undignified dash to the bathroom to freshen up after sex.

    In a bid to make life easier and less messy, the "come&gone" sponge promises to "quickly mop up all excess fluids" in minutes.

    Shaped a little bit like an ice lolly, the sponge is made from sterile, medical grade material and all you need to do is "insert, swirl, remove, and toss".

    Come&gone creator, Frances Tang, explained the sponge was created after she became fed up of "using toilet paper in an attempt to clean up".

    She added: "The more I spoke with other women about my problem, the product, and how ridiculous I thought it was that there was no hack for this, I realised I wasn’t alone.

    "Many women just felt too awkward and embarrassed to talk about it!

    "But, it just so happens that I am the one friend who lacks a filter and has an outrageous sense of humor, so...

    "Several iterations and one startup accelerator later, come&gone officially came to life.

    "The mission became clear - open up the conversation, normalise those awkward topics, and provide solutions for them.

    "I am so excited to present the come&gone sponge, and am even more eager to continue creating more products."

    And customers have so far been mega impressed, with several taking to the site to leave their reviews - with one even calling it a "necessity".

    One said: "The come&gone is amazing! This product makes the post-sex clean up so quick and so simple."

    While another added: "How have I lived without come&gone?! What a game-changer. Genius product!"

    The sponge is priced at £8 for a pack of 10, but sadly it doesn't look the product is available to ship to the UK just yet.
