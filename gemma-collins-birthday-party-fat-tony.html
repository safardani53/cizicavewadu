    IT'S safe to say that Gemma Collins knows how to throw a party after she pulled out all the stops for her 42nd birthday.

    The TV favourite let her hair down as she partied the night away with her closest friends and family.

    Gemma looked sensational in a glitzy black dress with sequin detail and a pink statement headband, which matched the ruffles on her extravagant frock.

    Looking glamourous for the special occasion, Gemma rocked bright fuchsia eyeshadow which complimented her rosy pink tresses which she styled into loose curls.

    The Essex babe kicked off her wild Friday night with live music from world famous DJ Fat Tony, real name Tony Marnach.

    Gemma shared a video with the legendary performer and gushed: "I've got the one and only DJ Fat Tony, he's coming to do my birthday gig, it's a dream come true."

    Fat Tony operates in an inner celebrity circle with the likes of Kate Moss and Boy George.

    Alongside a clip of the music star on the deck, Gemma wrote: "Can't wait for you to do the wedding."

    The party was in full swing with booze flowing, live music and plenty of guests taking to the dancefloor.

    Gemma's good pals and former X Factor stars Jedward also made an appearance, while her nephews Hayden and Kane were seen having a jolly time.
