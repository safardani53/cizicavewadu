    ACTOR Tyrese Gibson's mom, Pricilla Murray, has died after battling Covid-19 and pneumonia.

    The Fast and Furious actor, 43, has paid a touching tribute and said his family was "broken" without her.

    Who was Tyrese Gibson's mom Priscilla Murray Gibson?

    Well-recognized as Tyrese’s mother, Priscilla Murray Gibson first gained the attention of the public when her son debuted his 1998 studio album, which included the single Sweet Lady.

    The song hit number 12 on the US Billboard Hot 100.

    Priscilla also experienced alcoholism, as detailed in author Cynthia Banks’ book, Drunk For 27 Years.

    Drunk For 27 Years is the true story of Priscilla’s 27-year alcohol addiction and the heartbreak, pain, tragedy, and trauma she endured during that time. 

    Most read in Celebrity

    Priscilla was able to overcome her addiction with the aid of her children and the Alcoholics Anonymous Center in Hemet, California, and has been clean since September 23, 2006, according to the book.

    On Sunday, February 6, 2022, Tyrese shared that his mother was hospitalized for Covid-19 and pneumonia and being treated in the ICU.

    How did Priscilla Murray Gibson die?

    Tyrese had been keeping his fans and well-wishers updated on his mom's condition on social media.

    He regularly posted on Instagram with news, as well as asking for thoughts and prayers while Priscilla was in hospital.

    Tyrese praised his supporters for their “prayers, love, and energy" when he arrived at the hospital and uploaded a photo outside Murray's ICU bedside.

    Uploaded more photographs from the hospital where Murray was hospitalized, he wrote: "The Doctor sat in this chair and just broke my heart by telling me there's pretty much nothing else that can be done to turn things around. 

    "I paused, and in my most sincere voice I asked him…. You ever heard of a man name Jesus Christ? Nothing is impossible for him."

    Then he broke the devastating news on February 14, 2022, that she had died.

    Gibson captioned a heartbreaking video of himself holding his mom's hand in her hospital bed while crying: "On behalf of my family and everyone who ever spent anytime praying for my mother This is the saddest moment of my life………. My sweet Valentine just passed away….. 

    "May the lord Jesus Christ and his angels open the doors of heavens and embrace her………. We as a family are broken and just can't believe this……. May the lord Jesus Christ honor your walk with him and embrace you into the heavens……."

    He added: "From here on I ask that you HOLD MY HAND MOTHER and never let my hand go………. Like you held my hand as a child and a teen never let my hand go mother….."

    Who are Tyrese Gibson's siblings?

    Tyrese has three older siblings.

    He has one brother and two sisters.

    His elder brother is Tyrone Richardo. His two sisters are Salendra and Shonta. 

    Shonta is a radio personality, host, and writer. 

    She hosts a talk show named Morning Tea With The Queens.

    Her husband is called Eddie Bell.

    Salendra is married to an ex-marine William Charles Campbell III and they live in Escondido, California.

    Tyrese married his first wife Norma Mitchell in 2007 and they were divorced two years later having had one daughter together.
