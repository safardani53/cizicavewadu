    Inside Stassi Schroeder and fiance Beau Clarks $1.7M Hollywood Hills home featuring outdoor fount

    Vanderpump Rules star Stassi Schroeder has splashed out on a Hollywood Hills mansion with fianc Beau Clark. The couple, who are set to wed in Rome in October, announced the exciting news by sharing a snap outside of the Mediterranean-style property on Instagram.

    20 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Vanderpump Rules star Stassi Schroeder has splashed out on a Hollywood Hills mansion with fiancé Beau Clark.

    The couple, who are set to wed in Rome in October, announced the exciting news by sharing a snap outside of the Mediterranean-style property on Instagram.

    In the photograph, Stassi and Beau appear thrilled as they posed at the door of the mansion with their two dogs.

    The 31-year-old reality TV star looked casual in a sweater and leggings, while Beau wore a graphic-print sweater and jeans.

    The reality star captioned the post: "Homeowners AF."

    According to E! News, the couple purchased the Hollywood Hills home for $1.7 million.

    The property is the perfect pad for the soon-to-be newlyweds.

    In addition to panoramic canyon views, the mansion also boasts floor to ceiling windows and an outdoor patio.

    The huge kitchen area is complete with granite countertops and a built-in pizza oven.

    Meanwhile, the living room is the perfect spot for the lovers to unwind while catching up on the latest episodes of the hit Bravo show.

    The home also has an impressive light fixture in each room that gave every space its own personality.

    However, each room still felt cohesive and part of the same home.

    One of the best features of the couple's new home is the natural light and high ceilings which add the illusion of space to each room of the home.

    The couple has slowly been renovating the space to look more modern and fresh.

    A touch of new paint opened up the living room and gave it a more rustic look when they kept the darker woods.

    The home also features a stunning staircase in the backyard that guides them into the outdoor dining space decorated with hanging lights and stunning fountains.

    Earlier this week, Stassi's former best friend Kristen Doute lashed out at the star for dumping her since finding love with Beau.

    She told Us Weekly: "I hope that I’m wrong, but it kind of feels like as I’m watching these episodes, and it felt like over the summer, that Stassi was on this high horse of being in this now perfect relationship and now she’s getting married and everything’s great."
