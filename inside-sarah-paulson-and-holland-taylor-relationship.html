    Sarah Paulson and her boyfriend Holland Taylor are two of Hollywood’s greatest personalities. But they have more in common than simply a passion for acting. They started dating in 2015 and are still going strong. They are undeniably in love, and fans can’t get enough of them.

    Taylor just finished her off-Broadway performance, and Paulson paid homage to her longtime love.

    “Tonight is the last night of @hollandtaylor’s concert. I’ve loved her for seven years and counting, and today, as she prepares this show for landing, I love her even more. She is a true artist at heart. I adore geniuses. That is absolutely correct.”

    Paulson went on to praise her boyfriend, saying she was pleased to share her life with Taylor, whose creativity she admired. She concluded her article by saying she loved, appreciated, and worshiped her wife-like partner.

    Relationship Goals: Sarah Paulson and Holland Taylor

    The couple originally met at a dinner party in 2005, although they were both dating other people at the time. While her spouse was in a relationship, Paulson was seeing actress Cherry Jones. Despite the fact that they were with other individuals, it seemed like sparks flew between them. Paulson told The New York Times in 2016 that her companion was “the most absolutely gorgeous lady” she had ever seen.

    A decade later, the actresses crossed paths again, and this time they followed and DMed each other on Twitter. During an appearance on Watch What Happens Live With Andy Cohen in January 2019, the Ratched actress discussed how they met.

    “It’s a complicated tale.” We first met a long time ago. I was with someone else, and she was as well. We kind of passed one other by, then began following each other on Twitter, and then…” she recounted.

    “Holland Taylor snuck into your DMs?” questioned Billy Eichner, who was also a visitor. “She truly did, yes, it’s quite wonderful,” Paulson said.

    Holland Taylor and Sarah Paulson Confirm Their Relationship

    Taylor disclosed her connection with a lady younger than her in November 2015, although she did not divulge Paulson’s identity. A month later, the Two and a Half Men actor shared a selfie with Paulson, sparking conjecture. The pair did not announce their relationship until March 2016, when she talked about her sexuality in an interview with The New York Times.

    Paulson admitted she was “totally in love” with Taylor, but she found the attention and age gap in her relationship weird.

    “There’s something poignant about being with someone older. I believe there is better respect for time, what you have together, and what is essential, which might make the little things seem insignificant. “It casts a type of bright light blended with a kind of diffused light on things,” the actress said.

    Despite the fact that she was seeing a woman, Paulson said that she did not want to name her sexuality because it would make her feel confined, which she did not want.
