    LILY Allen was shocked to discover her favourite ice cream is not as fattening as she thought.

    The singer sent out a tweet asking her followers if they knew about the surprising number of calories that are in each cone.

    She wrote: “Is it true there’s only 190 calories in a cornetto? #cornettodiet”.

    And yesterday it was revealed the mum-of-three is the first celeb to sign an endorsement deal with the ice cream giant following her tweet last July.

    But do you know the calorie count of your favourite frozen treat?

    Here’s your guide to the good, the bad and the downright wicked of a selection of ice creams and lollies.

    1. The least calorific is ... the Mini Milk at just 30 calories

    2. Vimto Mixed Fruit Flavour Ices: 38 calories

    3. Ribena Ice Lolly: 43 calories

    4. Fruit Pastille Lolly: 58 calories

    5. Orange Calippo: 75 calories

    6. Twister: 76 calories

    7. Fab: 82 calories

    8. Solero Exotic: 104 calories

    9. Haagen-Dazs Strawberry Cheesecake mini tub: 235 calories

    10. 99 Flake cone: 240 calories

    11. Mars (67g bar): 249 calories

    12. Magnum Classic: 240 calories

    13. Feast: 293 calories

    14. McDonald's Wispa McFlurry: 410 calories

    15. And if you’ve had a really bad day, a whole tub of Ben & Jerry’s
    Chunky Monkey: 1,160 calories
