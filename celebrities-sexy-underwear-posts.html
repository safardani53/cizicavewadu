    HELEN FLANAGAN

    Posts: 30

    IT SADDENS me that actress and former Corrie girl Helen regularly gets mum-shamed when she posts sexy snaps on Instagram.

    Newsflash: Women can be mothers and enjoy a healthy sex life too.

    The 33-year-old’s daring lingerie snaps show women that we don’t have to hang up our kinky boots just because we have given birth.

    ASHLEY JAMES

    Posts: 31

    LIKE former Made In Chelsea star Ashley, I am also a 36-year-old mum of two, with our kids of similar ages – and I find her a massive inspiration.

    Not only does she look fantastic but I love her honest posts about motherhood and body positivity.

    She launched a DD+ range with Tu at Sainsbury’s at the beginning of the year and often shows the difficulties of finding sexy underwear for big boobs.

    Rating: 3

    ABBEY CLANCY

    Posts: 23

    MODEL Abbey looks every inch an Angel as she poses in Victoria’s Secret undies on Instagram.

    The 37-year-old mum of four also shares shots of more comfortable styles from VS’s loungewear collection.

    While Abbey’s sexy looks are absolutely incredible, her underwear posts can sometimes get just a little bit samey.

    I would maybe like to see her mix it up a little, with some daring sets and more colour.

    Rating: 3

    RIHANNA

    Posts: 12

    RIHANNA ripped up the rulebook when it came to pregnancy fashion, and I’m all for it.

    While most brands design frumpy outfits for expectant mums, the 35-year-old singer wore her bump with pride – often in sexy, risqué outfits.

    Earlier this year, she shared an adorable snap of her breastfeeding second son Riot Rose, wearing a black Fenty maternity bra.

    It looked stylish but also super- comfortable and practical.

    Rating: 4

    MEL B

    Posts: 14

    SPICE GIRL Mel has got an absolutely killer body and is ambassador for lingerie brand Pour Moi.

    She often poses with beloved daughter Phoenix, 24, in matching sets – showing how lingerie can look good on any woman, no matter what their age or shape.

    The 48-year-old also believes underwear isn’t just for the bedroom –and pairs sexy corsets with jeans for a more daring look when out and about.

    Rating: 4

    HEIDI KLUM

    Posts: 12

    LIKE Mel B, the 50-year-old supermodel often poses in lingerie with her 19-year-old daughter, Leni.

    Both are ambassadors for Intimissimi and look sensational in the ultra-feminine, lacy range.

    While the underwear is pretty, it’s more cutesy than sexy.

    It would be good to see Heidi rock a few more daring designs in 2024.

    Rating: 3

    KIM KARDASHIAN

    Posts: 22

    SHE’S used to causing controversy with her revealing outfits but reality TV queen Kim sent the internet into meltdown with her new faux nipple bra.

    Not only does the 43-year-old look stunning in everything she wears, but she gets top marks for her sense of humour too – with the viral video of her in the Skims nipple bra supposedly making a statement about climate change.

    Aside from showcasing her Skims range, she also posts sexy swimwear snaps of herself at various exotic holiday destinations.

    But it would be good to see a few more “real” posts from Kim – everything looks very curated.

    Rating: 3

    J-LO

    Posts: 25

    ACTRESS and singer J-Lo is flying the flag for older women with her age-defying body and attitude.

    She proves that you can still have a sizzling sex life in your fifties.

    The 54-year-old looked sensational when she showcased her new Intimissimi lingerie line This Is Me...Now.

    I love the lacy designs and intricate diamante detail, it’s the epitome of sexy and unapologetically feminine.
