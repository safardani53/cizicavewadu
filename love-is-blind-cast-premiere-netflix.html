    LOVE is Blind is a Netflix dating show where singles try and fall in love with people they have never seen face-to-face.

    The show debuted in February 2020 and is now back for its fifth season, causing fans to wonder more about the new cast members.

    Who is in the cast of Love is Blind season 5?

    The cast of Love is Blind season 5 includes:

    Aaliyah

    Aaliyah is an ICU travel nurse who is looking for a deeper connection.

    According to her bio, Aaliyah is looking for a foodie who she can develop a "pure and gentle" relationship with.

    Estefania

    Estefania is a teacher and dancer who is looking for a man who is caring and faithful.

    Shondra

    Shondra is a 32-year-old flight attendant who is searching for something that will "give her that spark."

    Maris

    Maris is an HR specialist who has only been in one serious relationship.

    Paige

    Paige is a stylist who is looking for someone with a "big heart, having genuine respect, and making a true commitment to each other."

    Renee

    Renee is a 32-year-old veterinarian.

    She spent most of her time focused on her career and is now ready to dip her toes back into the dating pool.

    Lydia

    Lydia is a geologist who is looking for the real thing.

    According to her bio, Lydia is "emotionally mature enough to be married."

    Erica

    Erica is a 27-year-old marketing manager who is tired of trying to find love on dating apps.

    She comes from parents who have a strong marriage and she hopes to have that someday.

    Miriam

    Miriam is a 32-year-old scientist in the pharmaceutical industry.

    Stacy

    Stacy is a director of operations who is looking for someone who is "family-oriented, outgoing, and driven."

    Johnie

    Johnie is a lawyer who claims to be "unlucky in love."

    Moving forward, Johnie is looking for someone who knows exactly what they're looking for.

    Linda

    Linda is a 32-year-old talent acquisition recruiter.

    She describes herself as someone who is picky when it comes to love but is hoping to find a "respectful, loving, and family-oriented" man.

    Mayra

    Mayra is a 25-year-old minister who is looking for a Ryan Reynolds lookalike who has a strong relationship with God like herself.

    Additionally, she is one of the first Hispanic ministers at her church Southern Baptist Convention.

    Taylor

    Taylor is a teacher who is on the hunt for a "soul connection."

    Robert

    Robert is a 30-year-old special education teacher who has been single for two years.

    Robert is now looking for someone who is "honest, loyal, and loving."

    Izzy

    Izzy is a salesman who is looking for a lady with good communication skills.

    Chris

    Chris is a 28-year-old project manager for commercial and retail development.

    Chris is hoping to put his previous love life in the past and hopes to find his life partner on the show.

    Efrain

    Efrain works in software sales and has become a dating app aficionado.

    This time around, Efrain hopes to settle down with an athletic girl who has a sense of humor.

    Ernesto

    Ernesto is a 32-year-old supply chain manager.

    After years of dating casually, Ernesto is ready to try and date at a more serious level.

    Connor

    Connor is a geoscientist who often puts his career ahead of his personal life.

    Conor is love looking for someone who is "smart, independent, and bilingual."

    Enoch

    Enoch is a 27-year-old financial advisor who's been single for five years and is on the hunt for someone who has a similar lifestyle as him.

    Jarred

    Jarred is a university director who is looking to "truly connect with someone and live life with mutual respect and compromise."

    Jared

    Jared is a 32-year-old firefighter from Houston.

    Jared is not a fan of finding his partner via apps and hopes Love Is Blind will match him with the real deal.

    Josh

    Josh is a sales representative who is passionate about finding a wife and eventually building a family with her.

    Carter

    Carter is a construction worker who is self-proclaimed to be a hopeless romantic.

    Justice

    Justice is a 28-year-old personal trainer.

    According to his bio, Justice is ready to "place love above everything else in my life."

    Milton

    Milton is a petroleum engineer who put his education and career first for years and is now getting back into the dating world.

    Uche

    Uche is a 34-year-old lawyer who has been single for two years.

    When did Love is Blind premiere season 5 premiere?

    Season 5 of Love is Blind kicked off on September 22, 2023.

    The show is set to release new episodes every Friday and will last for 10 episodes.

    Additionally, Nick and Vanessa Lachey reprised their role as hosts on the famed series.
