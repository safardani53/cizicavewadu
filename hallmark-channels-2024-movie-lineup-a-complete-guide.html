    January — Hallmark Channel

    ‘Love on the Right Course’

    Premiere Date: Saturday, January 6, at 8 p.m. ET.

    Stars: Ashley Newbrough and Marcus Rosner

    Logline: “A professional golfer (Newbrough) suffers a series of setbacks and returns home to Budapest to reevaluate her future in the sport. While there, a golf pro (Rosner) helps her rediscover herself and find love.”

    ‘A Scottish Love Scheme’

    Premiere Date: Saturday, January 13, at 8 p.m. ET.

    Stars: Erica Durance and Jordan Young

    Logline: “Lily (Durance) travels to Scotland with her mother and reconnects with Logan (Young), a childhood family friend. Unbeknownst to Lily and Logan, their meddling mothers have come up with a plan to set them up.”

    ‘Betty’s Bad Luck in Love’

    Premiere Date: Saturday, January 20, at 8 p.m. ET.

    Stars: Laci J Mailey and Marco Grazzini

    Logline: “Cursed from childhood to fail at romance, Betty's (Mailey) relationships have always ended in disaster. But when she meets Alex (Grazzini), she's tempted to try once more. Can true love prevail over a curse?”

    ‘Romance With a Twist’

    Premiere Date: Saturday, January 27, at 8 p.m. ET.

    Stars: Jocelyn Hudon and Oliver Renaud

    Logline: “Ex-dancer Luna (Hudon) pairs up with Bennett (Renaud), a world-class aerialist, when he finds himself without a partner for his upcoming silks performance for the local arts festival.”
