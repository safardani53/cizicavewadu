    LOVE Island star Millie Court has been forced to deny that she's had a boob job.

    Millie, 26, said she's been bombarded by fans asking if she has undergone any secret surgery lately.

    The reality star hit back and explained that she had put on 2st since the villa, making her boobs look bigger as a result.

    The fan asked: "Have you had your boobs done? They look amazing."

    Millie replied: "I feel like I've been getting this question a lot on my reels and like on Instagram and Snapchat. The answer to that is no, I haven't."

    She said her boobs change when her weight fluctuates - saying she dropped to a size 6 when she was in the villa, but has since returned to a size 10.

    "I feel like I've been very lucky when it comes to my boobs," Millie said on her latest YouTube video.

    "I've been blessed however when I lose weight, I lose weight on my bum and my boobs first which is really annoying as everyone just wants to lose weight off their belly and thighs.

    "But no, mine comes off my boobs.

    "When I was in Love Island, I lost two stone and I was a size six which for me, last time I was size six was probably 13 or 14.

    It comes after Millie confirmed that she has a new secret boyfriend - nine months after her split from Liam Reardon.

    Millie said other people's "judgement" was a big part why her last relationship ended and "struggled" with having a relationship in the public eye.

    "It’s early doors and you experienced a whole full-blown, meeting someone, relationship on TV and then everything afterwards," the Essex star continued.
