    "Vicki Gunvalson treated herself to what she called a detox retreat" getaway over the Oct. 3 weekend amid growing controversy over her ex-boyfriend Brooks Ayers.

    The Real Housewives of Orange County star, 53, took to Instagram to document her luxurious — and much needed — spa weekend at the Bacara Resort & Spa in Santa Barbara, Calif.

    On Saturday, Oct. 3, Gunvalson shared a photo of herself and three friends — including designer Marisa Kenson — on the first night of her retreat.

    "The start of our detox retreat at @bacararesortsb with @skyranchfitness and @clubdetox_oc. Yoga, meditation, hiking, massage and healthy eating is what's in store for me this weekend," she captioned the photo that showed her looking happy in a black evening dress.

    The next day, the RHOC star showed off a snap of how she's eating healthy while at the retreat. "Great way to start the morning before my hike @bacararesorts, @clubdetox_oc, @marisakenson, #healthyeating, #freshberries, #detox, #juicing," she captioned the image of a bowl of berries and a bottle of juice.

    Later that afternoon, Ayers' ex shared a photo of her holding a Club Detox green juice, wearing a Club Detox T-shirt.

    "I love detoxing and spending this weekend with people that inspire me and believe in me," she wrote. "Thank you ladies for allowing me a stress free day. #clubdetoxoc #juicing #cleansingyourmindandbody."

    Gunvalson has been at the center of drama with her fellow Housewives over her ex-boyfriend Ayers. Meghan King Edmonds has been trying to disprove Ayers' claim that he is suffering from non-Hodgkin’s lymphoma.

    Ayers recently spoke at length to Us Weekly about claims from Gunvalson's RHOC costars that he has been faking a cancer diagnosis — especially claims from Edmonds.

    "Meghan has scoffed that, 'Oh my God, Brooks is threatening to sue me.' I have significant grounds if she doesn't change her ways. This is defamation," he told Us

    Ayers said on Oct. 1 that despite the claims that he'd faked his cancer diagnosis after he was spotted partying in Fort Lauderdale, he was not on chemotherapy treatment then.
