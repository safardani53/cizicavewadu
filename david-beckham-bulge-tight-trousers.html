    DAVID Beckham set pulses racing as he wore a pair of tight trousers on a family holiday to Italy.

    The 44-year-old footie legend attracted a lot of attention thanks to his large bulge as he posed alongside wife Victoria, who shared the picture of them.

    Taking to Instagram to comment on the snap, one person asked: “No one mentioning David's massive bulge ? Just me ? What a bloke.”

    While another wrote: “Nice package Becks!”

    And someone else cheekily remarked: “Is it just me but is there a budgie smuggling?”

    Posh and Becks, nicknamed Golden Balls by his wife, have been keeping fans updated during their trip to Puglia by sharing lots of pictures.

    Just yesterday they posted a picture wearing matching outfits, which Posh captioned: "#Twinning all summer. I had it on first just to be clear."

    The couple have been joined by their kids Brooklyn, Romeo, Cruz and Harper on the holiday.

    The vacation comes just a week after it was revealed the Beckhams have bought an incredible £36million luxury apartment in Miami.

    The couple were recently spotted visiting the One Thousand Museum in the Scorpion building in downtown Miami and now Marca reports the superstar couple has splashed out on a penthouse.

    The incredible building, designed by architect Zaha Hadid, has a rooftop pool, spa, hair salon, juice bar and a gym.

    More on Posh and Becks

    According to the website prices start at £5m and go up to £45m.

    The Beckhams viewed the property on a family holiday, also visiting a waterpark with daughter Harper and hanging out with celeb mates Eva Longoria and Marc Anthony.

    David is moving part-time to Miami in the run up to launching his Major League Soccer team in the city, called Inter Miami.
