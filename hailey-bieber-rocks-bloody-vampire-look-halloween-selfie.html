    HAILEY Bieber has promoted her skincare brand's lip tint while dressed as a bloody vampire in the spirit of Halloween.

    The wife of Justin Bieber was seen taking a series of selfies while applying the dark red lip color to resemble blood.

    On Instagram, Hailey, 26, promoted Rhode Skin's new Raspberry Jelly tint as it was seen dripping down the sides of her mouth and painted onto her neck and chest.

    To add to her vampire look, the model added in a pair of faux fangs and wore a layered pearl choker.

    She painted some red strokes onto her hands to appear as blood and wore an off-the-shoulder costume top to complete her ensemble.

    Hailey, who recently addressed rumors of a potential pregnancy, had her hair tied back into an intricate updo to fully show off her dramatic makeup look.
