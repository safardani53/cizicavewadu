    What chocolates are in a Quality Street tub?

    AS Christmas approaches, supermarket shelves are set to be stocked with festive favourite Quality Street tubs. But what chocolates can you enjoy and what is the most popular pick of the tub? Here's everything you need to know.

    27 Feb 2024, 00:00

    AS Christmas approaches, supermarket shelves are set to be stocked with festive favourite Quality Street tubs.

    But what chocolates can you enjoy and what is the most popular pick of the tub? Here's everything you need to know.

    What chocolates are in a Quality Street tub?

    If you're planning on picking up a Quality Street tub or two to enjoy over the festive season then you may notice a slight change.

    In October 2022, Nestle announced Quality Street will axe its iconic brightly coloured wrappers over 86 years since their release.

    The move came after the chocolate brand wanted to become more environmentally friendly, with the treats now being wrapped in a duller form of waxed paper, which is recyclable.

    Only the two foil-covered sweets - the green triangle and orange crunch will remain in their original packaging.

    However, what hasn't changed is the choice of chocolate up for grabs.

    Here's everything you need to know about what chocolates are in a Quality Street tub.

    The Purple One

    First up is The Purple One.

    This fan favourite chocolate contains a whole roasted hazelnut, wrapped in runny caramel and encased in a milk chocolate shell.

    The Green Triangle

    Next up is The Green Triangle.

    This Quality Street chocolate is made with a smooth hazelnut and is coated in a delicious layer of chocolate.

    Orange Crème

    If you're a lover of orange chocolate, then this Quality sweet is right up your street.

    The Orange Crème guarantees a tangy soft orange flavour, whilst being coated in dark chocolate.

    Toffee Finger

    If you prefer a chewier treat - then Quality Street have the perfect option for you.

    The Toffee finger is included as one of the 12 sweet treats in the tub and is made with toffee and milk chocolate.

    Milk Chocolate Block

    The Milk Chocolate Block can be spotted wrapped in a green paper.

    This tasty treat is simple but sweet and is made with a solid block of smooth and creamy milk chocolate.

    Strawberry Delight

    The chocolate company refer to this treat as their ''red Quality Street''.

    It is made with a strawberry flavoured fondant and wrapped in rich dark chocolate.

    Fudge

    If you dig in the tub and come across the pink chocolates, then you've found yourself a festive fudge treat.

    This chocolate is made with creamy fudge and is wrapped in milk chocolate.

    Orange Crunch

    The second orange chocolate on the menu is the Orange Crunch.

    This chocolate is made with orange truffle and crunchy orange pieces.

    Toffee Penny

    One of the most famous chocolates to be added to the tub is the Toffee Penny.

    This treat promises to be chewy, golden and made with quality toffee.

    Caramel Swirl

    If you like The Purple One then the odds are this chocolate will be one you enjoy.

    Also containing runny caramel, this sweet treat is made with wrapped in a milk chocolate cup.

    Coconut Eclair

    The next one on the list is the Coconut Eclair.

    This chocolate is made with chewy coconut covered in milk chocolate.

    Lemon Zing

    And last but not least is the new Lemon Zing chocolate.

    Created as part of the spring pouch, this new treat contains lemon crème and dark chocolate.

    What is the most popular Quality Street?

    Households up and down the country debate their favourite Quality Street treat every year - but which one is the most popular?

    According to Nestle in 2016, the nation's favourite Quality Street chocolate was The Purple One.

    And it seems that the nation has stood by their choice ever since - with The Purple One still being voted as fan in 2018.
