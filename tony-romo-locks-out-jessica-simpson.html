    Tony Romo Locks Out Jessica Simpson

    After Tony Romo broke up with her the night before her 29th birthday, he took extra precautions in shielding himself from Jessica Simpson in Dallas. Specifically, he made sure she wouldnt be able to get near his home.

    08 Jul 2024, 00:00

    After Tony Romo broke up with her the night before her 29th birthday, he took extra precautions in shielding himself from Jessica Simpson in Dallas.

    Specifically, he made sure she wouldn’t be able to get near his home.

    A sign at the entrance of his gated community reads: “RED ALERT!!! TONY ROMO HAS MADE SOME CHANGES TO HIS LIST OF PEOPLE ALLOWED IN… JESSICA SIMPSON IS NO LONGER ON THE LIST AND NOT APPROVED FOR ACCESS.”

    Red alert?

    Seems a bit extreme. Maybe the John Mayer text messages set him off.

    Not that Jessica wanted to stick around: The singer paid $19,000.00 on July 15 to ship 37 boxes worth of her belongings from Dallas to L.A. overnight.

    Couples often break up and say they don’t want to see each other anymore. But Tony Romo really doesn’t want to see Jessica Simpson anymore. [Photo: Fame]

    After the the couple – who began dating in November 2007 – called it quits earlier this month, a pal of Romo said that “It’s been a long time coming.”

    Not everyone expected the news, however.

    “I thought they would… tie the knot,” said Romo’s insane former teammate Terrell Owens, who famously slammed Jess for being a “jinx” on America’s Team.

    For her part, Simpson has been working out again with a personal trainer in L.A., going on movie dates with her mom and girlfriends, and trying to move on.

    “Everyone needs to know hope floats… grab the strings and pull it back to you… Falling asleep with my mom and the dogs. Please, Lord, give all of my beautiful fans, friends, enemies, and family rest. Bring all of us peace,” she Tweeted.

    That is pretty pathetic. No wonder her family feels bad for Jessica.
