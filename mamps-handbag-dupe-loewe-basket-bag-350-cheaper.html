    WE all love the look of designer items - but let's be honest, the price tag isn't as appealing.

    But if you've been eyeing up the Loewe basket bag then you're in luck, as M&S is stocking the perfect dupe and it's £350 cheaper than the original.

    Fashion fans have taken to TikTok, urging shoppers to snap up the £45 bag in stores.

    TikToker @thestylcoach, shared a clip of the bag on her account, writing: "BATTLE OF THE BASKET BAGS LOEWE vs MARKS & SPENCER."

    Sarah had both bags so she could accurately compare them, and it seemed the only difference was that the M&S dupe was a few inches larger than its designer counterpart.

    Another style whizz, @missyocd_12, shared the news that the bags had been released again this year for summer.

    READ MORE ON M&S

    And she was quick to advise shoppers to pick it up before it sells out again.

    She wrote: "Dupe for the Loewe.

    "Now in stock - run before they all get sold out like last year."

    The M&S and Loewe bag both have a straw body - hence the name 'basket bag.'

    Most read in Fabulous

    The two bags also come with leather handles and a leather front pocket on the front of the bag for an extra luxe look.

    One small difference in the bags is that the M&S version comes with a white cloth bag inside that can be done up to keep your goods secure whereas the Loewe version doesn't.

    But the price difference between the two is the real kicker.

    M&S is currently selling the bag for £45, while the Loewe one is an eye-watering £395.

    But you better be quick if you want to get your hands on it, M&S released the same bag last year for summer and it quickly sold out.

    Fashion fans took to TikTok and couldn't wait to get their hands on them.

    One person wrote: "Saw this in M&S yesterday and loved it."

    "I gasped with excitement when I seen them," replied Sarah.

    Another commented: "Cute bags!"
