    THE Wednesday night Powerball drawing had a jackpot of $35million.

    Here's everything you need to know about the winning numbers and how much cash the winner of the lottery game takes home.

    What were the winning numbers for Wednesday's Powerball drawing?

    The winning numbers for the Wednesday, August 10, 2022 draw are: 29, 44, 59, 61, 68 and Powerball 19 with a Power Play of 2X.

    How much was the Powerball prize for August 10?

    The prize was an estimated $35million, with a cash value of $20.6million.

    The Powerball is drawn every Monday, Wednesday, and Saturday night at 10.59pm ET.

    You can see the winning numbers on the Powerball.com Livestream or the LotteryHUB app on iPhone or Android every time there's a drawing.

    How much is a ticket and where can Powerball be played?

    Powerball tickets cost $2 per play.

    The game can be played in nearly every US state, including Washington DC, Puerto Rico, and the US Virgin Islands, with the exceptions of Alabama, Alaska, Hawaii, Nevada, and Utah.

    How do you play Powerball?

    Players select five numbers from 1 to 69; and one Powerball number from 1 to 26. 

    All players must match all five white balls and the red Powerball in order to win the jackpot.
