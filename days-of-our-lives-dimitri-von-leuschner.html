    Who plays Dimitri Von Leuschner on Days of Our Lives?

    Dimitri Von Leuschner is played by Peter Porte; he became a series regular during Days of Our Lives' 58th season.

    Born on March 31, 1984, Porte is an American actor from Greenwood Lake, New York.

    He earned a following from soap opera viewers after he started appearing as Dimitri during Days of Our Lives: Beyond Salem.

    Porte is also known for his stint as Ricky Williams on The Young and The Restless.

    "I thought they were throwing me in for another episode and then they offered the contract and I was shocked," Porte told Soap Opera Digest.

    "I always wanted to come back because I think it is the closest to live theater that we have on this coast, and that’s where I got my start.

    "I was shocked and so excited. I’m very excited for the viewers just to see how many twists the storyline of Dimitri von Leuschner goes through in a very short amount of time.

    "The speed at which you are thrust into the pool and expected to perform is intoxicating and it’s nerve-racking.

    "Without giving away too much, I will say this...I was walking into this clueless because there’s a hard pivot and we introduced who this character really is for three pages of dialogue."

    Who is Dimitri on DAYS?

    On DAYS: BS, Dimitri Von Leuschner initially went under the guise of ISA agent Kyle Graham.

    As the spinoff's first season concluded, it was revealed Agent Graham was actually Carly Manning and Frankie Brady's cousin Dimitri.

    After his plan to extort multiple countries by using a stolen and much-sought-after relic was thwarted, he was taken into police custody.

    Dimitri returned during the show's 58th season; he surprised Chad DiMera and Steve Johnson when he revealed he was the one who kidnapped Kate Roberts.

    How can I watch DAYS?

    From Monday to Friday, new episodes of DAYS are available daily via Peacock.

    From its premiere in November 1965 to September 2022, the long-running series was aired on NBC.

    On August 3, 2022, the network and the show announced its decision to switch platforms from NBC to Peacock.
