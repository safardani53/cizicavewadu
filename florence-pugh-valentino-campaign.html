    FLORENCE Pugh signs on the polka dotted line to be the new face of Maison Valentino.

    The Don’t Worry Darling actress, 27, wore this spotty blouse while modelling a bag from the new range.

    She gave very expressive poses while showing off the designer gear and admitted she was “ecstatic” to have landed the job.

    Florence wrote on Instagram: “As you can see.. I’m pretty shocked and ecstatic to announce that my Maison Valentino campaign is here.

    “Truly an honour to continue creating art with my family at Valentino. Working with talented people is one thing, but to work with talented people who are great friends feeds the soul!

    “This campaign truly did feed the soul. Thank you for letting me be me.”

    The actress starred alongside singer Harry Styles, 29, in the film Don’t Worry Darling - directed by his now ex Olivia Wilde, 38.

     The Sun told how Harry secretly kissed Florence on the set of their film — weeks before he began a relationship with the director, Olivia.

    The snog is understood to be the real reason the two women fell out while filming.

    In her only interview to promote the 1950s-set thriller, she appeared to take aim at Olivia for using an intimate scene in the trailer for the film.
