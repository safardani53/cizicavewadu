    Kendall Jenner Raves About Newborn Niece Dream Kardashian

    Living in a Dream! Kendall Jenner gushed over her newborn niece, Dream Kardashian, and said that her brotherRob Kardashianis happier than ever after welcoming his first child with fiance Blac Chyna.

    Living in a Dream! Kendall Jenner gushed over her newborn niece, Dream Kardashian, and said that her brother Rob Kardashian is happier than ever after welcoming his first child with fiancée Blac Chyna.

    “She’s a dream. She’s so beautiful. I went and met her the day [she was born],” Jenner, 21, told Ellen DeGeneres, adding that she believes the infant bears a striking resemblance to Rob’s late father. “She looks just like Robert Kardashian.”

    “She kinda does,” the talk show host agreed. “Well, she looks like Rob.”

    Jenner went on to say that first-time fatherhood has had a very positive effect on her formerly reclusive sibling.

    “I mean, it’s crazy. She’s so pretty and [Rob is] so happy,” the model said of the Arthur George sock designer, 29. “It makes me so happy I could literally cry. He’s, like, the cutest.”

    Since welcoming Dream on Thursday, November 10, Rob and Chyna, 28 — who is also mom of King Cairo, 4, with ex-fiancé Tyga — have taken to social media to share their excitement over staring a family together.

    Hours after his baby girl was born, the sole male Kardashian sibling told fans that he also sees the physical similarities between Dream and his dad, who passed away in 2003 at age 59 of esophageal cancer.
