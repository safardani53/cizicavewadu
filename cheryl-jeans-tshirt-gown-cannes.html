    02 Sep 2024, 00:00

    CHERYL looked relaxed in a casual look of jeans and a white T-shirt as she stepped out in Cannes on Saturday.

    Last night Cheryl dazzled in white as she hit the red carpet for the premiere of Ash Is The Purest White.

    The 34-year-old jetted in to the French Riviera yesterday to make her first appearance at the Cannes film festival since giving birth to son Bear in 2017.

    Cheryl's look had a 70s vibe as she sported light blue denim flares which she paired with a round neck plain white T-shirt.

    She added a pop of colour with a bright yellow cardigan and some matching yellow shades.

    The former X Factor judge, who is at Cannes as a Representative for L’Oréal, smiled for the cameras made her way to Martinez beach.

    Her relaxed look comes after Cheryl opted for a sheer white gown for a premiere on Friday night.

    Cheryl walked the red carpet alone, while Liam Payne was presumably at work.

    The bridal-like dress featured a delicate high neck with beaded detailing, while the stunning skirt showed off her legs.

    Her long brown hair fell all the way down her back and was pulled off her face slightly, showing off natural beauty,  which she accentuated with a dash of red lipstick.
