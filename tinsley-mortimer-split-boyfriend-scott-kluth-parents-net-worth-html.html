    Real Housewives of New York (RHONY) star Tinsley Mortimer and the CEO of money-saving site CouponCabin Scott Kluth's relationship has again faced hindrance.

    The couple reportedly broke up, and sources to the US Weekly claimed that the reason for their split was their distance and hectic schedule. Tinsley resides in New York while her boyfriend is hundreds of miles away in Chicago, adding the stress on their distance problem. 

    One of the sources to the US Weekly reported, 

    They have a complicated relationship. They talk every single day and still say I love you. She’s so busy in NYC and he’s so busy in Chicago that they are not exclusive right now because it’s long distance. 

    Though the pair has broken up without spilling any words, this was not the first time of their parting ways. The two had first halted their relationship back in October 2017. 

    However,  the duo rebounded after Scott returned to the Bravo's reality show in 2018's season. Since then the two experienced ups and downs in their relationship and broke their relationship several times.

    But when the two last patched up during the episode of RHONY in May 2018, Tinsley showed her concern to marry Scott and be parents together.

    Tinsley, who owns a net worth of $35 million, even went on to try wedding dress with her mom Dale in one of the recent episodes. She tried the dress called The Clara worth $5K and the Kimpton worth $10,230 before face timing with her fertility doctor, who revealed the pictures of her seven frozen eggs.  

    Watching her potential hopes of being a mother, Tinsley and her mother teared up and hinted that she desperately wanted to be a parent.

    While Tinsley and Scott have low-keyness in their split, there are hopes for the two to rekindle. One of the insiders to US Weekly quoted,

    "They’ve done this before, these little breaks. They are both super optimistic they will be able to work it out when their schedules aren’t as crazy … They love each other so much.”

    Also, another source stated, 

    Scott can’t let go of Tinsley emotionally. Tinsley is hanging on, hoping that Scott will agree to marry her.

    Tinsley and Scott first met during season 9 of the Bravo series RHONY when they were introduced to one another by co-star Carole Radziwill in 2017.

    Tinsley Mortimer celebrated Labor's Day with boyfriend Scott Kluth in Montenegro on 3 September 2017 (Photo: Tinsley Mortimer's Instagram)

    Before being in a relationship with Scott, Tinsley was married to Robert Livingston Mortimer from 2002 to 2010. The duo, who met at Lawrenceville School, eloped at the age of 18 but married only when they were 26.

    As of now, her ex-husband is married to shoe designer Tabitha Simmons since 9 June 2018.
