    Judy Zebra "J. Z." Knight is an American spiritual teacher and author known for transforming her channel into a spiritual entity named Ramtha. Knight has appeared on American television shows, such as Larry King, MSNBC, and The Merv Griffin Show, as well as in various media such as Psychology Today. His teachings have attracted figures from entertainment and political circles such as Linda Evans and Shirley MacLaine. Knight has vowed to link ancient wisdom and the power of consciousness to the latest scientific discoveries. Some ideas, similar to those of Shirley MacLaine, have been criticized by mathematician and skeptic Martin Gardner as "kindergarten metaphysics." Shirley Maclaine claims in her book that she was Ramtha's brother in their past Atlantean life. Ramtha's teachings have been criticized by scientists and skeptics. The Southern Poverty Law Center criticized Knight for his "racist, anti-Catholic anti-Semitism."
