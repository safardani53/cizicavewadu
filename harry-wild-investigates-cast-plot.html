    Harry Wild Investigates cast, plot, release date: All about the Channel 5 crime drama

    JANE Seymour is back to her TV roots, as she leads the cast in Harry Wild Investigates. The Channel 5 murder mystery series sees the legendary actress play a retired uni professor turned super-sleuth.

    01 Oct 2024, 00:00

    JANE Seymour is back to her TV roots, as she leads the cast in Harry Wild Investigates.

    The Channel 5 murder mystery series sees the legendary actress play a retired uni professor turned super-sleuth.

    When is Harry Wild Investigates on Channel 5?

    Harry Wild Investigates kicks off on Channel 5 on February 25, 2023 at 8pm.

    Episodes in this eight part series will running weekly on Saturday nights at the same time, concluding on April 15, 2023.

    You can also catch up with episodes after they have aired on the streaming service My5.

    While this show is new to Channel 5 for 2023, Harry Wild Investigates previously launched worldwide on Acorn TV in spring 2022.

    Reda more on Channel 5 drama

    What is Harry Wild Investigates about?

    Murder mystery thriller Harry Wild Investigates follows Harriet 'Harry' Wild, a recently retired university professor who discovers she has a knack for solving crimes.

    The series of the drama follows Harry as she finds herself at a crossroads after she becomes a victim of a mugging and decides to recover at the home of her police detective son, Charlie.

    But once under Charlie’s roof, Harry soon starts interfering in his latest murder investigation.

    Who is in the cast of Harry Wild Investigates?

    Jane Seymour

    Legendary actress Jane Seymour takes on the lead role of Harry.

    Most read in Drama

    Her character is described by Channel 5 as a retired professor, who is also a bit of a "foul-mouthed, whiskey-loving drinker" .

    Jane is best known for the 90s TV show, Dr Quinn, Medicine Woman, that had millions of fans around the world.

    She was also one of the original Bond girls.

    Speaking about playing Harry, Jane told Channel 5: "From the first moment I read the script, I fell in love with Harry Wild.

    "I’ve loved stepping into the role of this very curious and passionate retired professor."

    Rohan Nedd plays Fergus Reid

    Harry’s sidekick Fergus Reid is played by Rohan Nedd.

    The two characters didn’t meet on the best of terms, with Fergus mugging Harry.

    But after seeing the potential in the teen, she takes him on as her apprentice in investigating.

    Actor Rohan’s first role was in Doctor Who in 2017.

    Since then he has starred in the Netflix series Safe

    Kevin Ryan plays DI Charlie Wild

    Harry’s son is played by Kevin Ryan.

    Charlie is a senior detective, who doesn’t appreciate his mother meddling in his cases.

    Actor Kevin is best known for starring in Raw, and BBC's Guilt.

    He also appeared on the BBC America series Copper, playing Detective Francis Maguire.

    Amy Huberman plays Orla

    Actress Amy Huberman stars as Orla, Charlie’s wife.

    Her character is described as being "slightly overbearing but means well".

    Actress Amy is known for starring in Cold Feet, and playing the lead in Finding Joy.
