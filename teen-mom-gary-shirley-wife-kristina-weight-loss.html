    GARY Shirley and his wife Kristina have debuted their dramatic weight loss transformations in a new clip from the Teen Mom: Next Chapter reunion.

    The MTV couple has been working hard to shed the pounds in recent months and focus on their health.

    Now Gary, 36, and his wife Kristina, also 36, have shown off the results of their efforts.

    The reality star and his spouse appeared on the Teen Mom: Next Chapter reunion alongside his ex Amber Portwood, 33, and the ex's daughter Leah, 14, this week.

    The TV personality spoke about how he supported his teenage daughter's decision to begin taking birth control and how he has chosen to "trust" her to make the correct decisions.

    The little girl then thanked her mom, dad, and step-mom for their support, leaving Amber in tears over her daughter's decision to forgive her for past mistakes.

    Meanwhile, Kristina sported a skin-tight black cocktail dress and sparkly silver heels, showing off her newly fit physique.
